Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Pulse is built around one idea: habit tracking should feel good, not stressful. Every feature is designed to support your journey — not overwhelm it.

✅

Habit Tracking & Streaks

Check off habits daily, build streaks, and set weekly goals. Filter by Health, Productivity, or Mindfulness — track what matters to you.

📊

Deep Analytics

12-week heatmap, day-of-week breakdown, and per-habit performance. See your patterns and understand when you perform best.

📅

Calendar View

See your entire month at a glance with color-coded days. Track mood alongside habits and spot your best weeks.

🌿

AI Habit Coach

Ask your personal coach anything about building habits. Get science-backed answers and interactive exercises to make habits stick faster.

🎯

Life Goals

Set big life goals and break them into milestones. Track numeric progress — km run, books read, money saved. Never lose sight of what matters.

🧘

Built-in Meditations

Morning energy, stress relief, focus sessions, and evening wind-down. 5 guided meditations built right in — no extra apps needed.

🏆

Achievements & Badges

Unlock 16 unique badges as you grow. From your first habit to 100-day streaks — every milestone deserves recognition.

👥

Community & Friends

Share your progress, follow friends, and get inspired by others. Celebrate wins together and stay accountable.

😊

Mood Tracking

Log how you feel each day and discover which habits make you happiest. See the patterns others miss.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Set custom reminders for each habit at the perfect time. Never forget what you committed to — Pulse has your back.

🔒

100% Private

No account required. All your data stays on your device. Pulse works completely offline — your habits are yours alone.

🌍

3 Languages

Fully available in English, Russian, and Dutch. The best habits happen in your language, with your mindset.

How it works

Ready in
2 minutes.

No complicated setup. No onboarding questionnaire. Just open the app and start building the life you want.

01
Add your first habit
Type any habit, choose a colour and icon, set your weekly goal, and optionally add a daily reminder. That's it — you're done with setup.
02
Check in every day
Open Pulse, tap the circle next to your habit. Satisfying, instant, done. Your week fills up with colour as you go.
03
Track your mood
A quick daily mood check-in takes 2 seconds. Over time, Pulse shows you exactly how your habits affect how you feel.
04
Celebrate your wins
Unlock badges, hit milestones, share your progress with friends. Pulse makes every small victory feel earned.
